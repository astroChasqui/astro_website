Hello, my name is Ivan Ramirez. I am an astronomer at The University of Texas at Austin and McDonald Observatory. My research focuses on high resolution stellar spectroscopy, in particular observational studies of stellar atmospheres, fundamental parameters, and chemical composition of Sun-like stars. Applications of my work range from the search and characterization of stars that harbor extrasolar planets to understanding the mechanisms of galaxy formation and evolution.
